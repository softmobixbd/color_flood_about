About Color Flood

Use strategy, Match colors, and Flood It!

Color Flood is a minimalistic yet highly addictive puzzle game crafted to test your strategic thinking and color-matching skills. Developed by SoftMobiXBD, this game challenges players to flood a colorful grid into a single color within a limited number of moves.

Gameplay Overview

Start with a randomized grid filled with vibrant colors. Your mission is to turn the entire grid into one single color by gradually expanding from the top-left cell—color by color—within a fixed number of moves. The larger the grid, the tougher the challenge!

Difficulty Levels

Color Flood offers four unique difficulty levels:

  • Easy – Smaller grid, more forgiving.
  • Medium – A balanced challenge.
  • Hard – Requires sharp strategy.
  • Extreme – For true puzzle masters.

Each level increases the grid size from 13x13 up to 22x22, and players are given 20 to 40 moves, depending on the level.

Who Can Play?

Color Flood is designed for players aged 13 and up. Whether you're a casual gamer or someone who enjoys thoughtful strategy games, Color Flood delivers a satisfying and relaxing gameplay experience.

Free to Play

The Color Flood is completely free. It includes banner ads on menus, rewarded ads for have extra moves when you want to continue after losing. There are no in-app purchases.
