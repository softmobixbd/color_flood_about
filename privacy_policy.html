Privacy Policy for Color Flood

Effective Date: July 15, 2025

At SoftMobiXBD, your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy outlines how the Color Flood app handles your data.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal or sensitive user data. The app does not require user registration or login.

2. Third-Party Services

To enhance functionality and support free access to the game, Color Flood uses the following third-party services:

  • AdMob: for displaying ads and monetization.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: for push notifications.

These services may collect information as governed by their own privacy policies.

3. Local Data Storage

Color Flood stores non-personal gameplay data on your device using SharedPreferences:

  • Best win count
  • Best lose count

This data remains local and is never shared externally.

4. Data Deletion

Since no user data is collected or stored on external servers, there is no requirement or mechanism to request deletion.

5. International Use

Color Flood is available globally and adheres to this uniform privacy policy across all regions.
